ChoresDone

Manage Chores. Make them easy.

A Simple Household management app. Add people. Add Tasks. Nudge people to complete their tasks.

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

⊕Why did you make ChoresDone?

I wanted a simple way to track chores for a household. When I couldn't find an app that does that online, I decided to make it.

⊕What do the checkboxes mean?

Each checkbox represents a chore for a person on a specific day of the week. Tick them when it's done!

⊕Will everything stay If I refresh the page?

Yes, everything will stay. The site saves all chores done locally on the browser that you're using. However, the data Is deleted when you log-out.

⊕Can I delete a chore or person?

Yes, you can. Click on the trash icon next to the person or chore column to delete them.

⊕Is my data safe? Are you collecting anything?

Your data is safe. Everything happens in your browser.
